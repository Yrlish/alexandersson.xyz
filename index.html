WorkLog

WorkLog is made for people working in multiple projects and needs to keep track of all time spent on each project, if you for example need to fill in time reports for your employer on what you work on.

Instead of writing it all down into a text file, spreadsheet or similar and calculate together all hours per day per project by yourself, you write in a start time and stop time for a working session and WorkLog summarizes it all and presents it nicely for you.
